Coinhabit

researcher, UX/UI, product designer

challenge: How to improve the financial health of people who live in co-living?

a weekend, a challenge

I carried out this project in the selection process of BBVA's Future Designers VI program during the development phase of the practical design challenge.

To do this, they shared a briefing with us with clear guidelines to follow, its theme and the type of deliverable they wanted. The challenge to which we had to provide a solution in just 72 hours.

Let's start at the beginning...

We found that residents may have different purchasing power

Because co-living attracts a wide range of people, there is great economic diversity among its residents, making it difficult to share expenses equitably. Therefore, it raises the need to establish a financial balance that is fair for everyone.

Fuente: Observatorio de emancipación
The final solution

An app, a podcast, a brand

It was considered important to help them manage their money easily and equitably, taking into account each person's capacity. Simplifying the effort, educating financially and creating a brand that helps create community.
